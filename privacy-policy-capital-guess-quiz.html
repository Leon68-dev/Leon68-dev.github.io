Privacy Policy for Capital Guess Quiz

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "Capital Guess Quiz" ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by SoftDevelopUA.

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Information collected automatically (Third-party services)

To provide advertisements, process payments, and improve stability, the Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising): Used to display ads. AdMob may collect and use technical data, including your device’s Advertising ID and IP address, to show relevant advertisements.

  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics): Used to monitor performance and app usage. It collects device identifiers, crash reports, and technical statistics to help us improve the user experience.

  • Google Play Billing: Used to process in-app purchases (e.g., "Remove Ads"). All payment transactions are handled securely by Google Play. We do not have access to your credit card or banking information.

3. Data Collected by Third Parties

The technical information collected automatically may include:

  • The type of mobile device and operating system.

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, Installation UUID).

  • Interaction data (ad views, clicks, and app usage patterns).

  • Crash reports and stack traces for debugging purposes.

4. Does the Application collect precise real-time location information?

No, this Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Third-party services (AdMob) are configured to comply with applicable privacy regulations and Google Play’s families policy where applicable.

6. Third parties and Data Sharing

We share only aggregated, anonymized data with the service providers mentioned above. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via email at softdevelop.ua@gmail.com.
